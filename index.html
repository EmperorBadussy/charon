02 // FEATURES

The Ferryman's Arsenal

Every tool needed to carry music across the digital stream. Engineered for efficiency.

TIDAL SEARCH ENGINE
Search artists, albums, tracks. Full metadata, cover art, quality badges. Browse discographies.
MASTER QUALITY DOWNLOADS
MQA/Hi-Res, FLAC CD, AAC 320, AAC 96. You choose the quality. tiddl handles the rest.
DOWNLOAD QUEUE
Queue unlimited downloads. 3 concurrent by default. Configurable. Persistent across restarts.
NAVIDROME SERVER MANAGER
Install, start, stop, restart Navidrome from within CHARON. Auto-download latest release. Config editor.
AUTO LIBRARY SCAN
Every download triggers a Navidrome scan. Your music appears in AETHER within seconds.
ARTIST DISCOGRAPHY
One click downloads an artist's entire catalog. Every album queued automatically.
AETHER COMPANION
Built as the supply chain for AETHER. Rip, Organize, Play. The complete pipeline.
